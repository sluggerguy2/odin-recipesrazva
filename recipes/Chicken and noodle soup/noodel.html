Chicken and noodle soup

Description

A chicken noodle soup recipe that is very easy and fast

Ingredients

  • 3 (14.5 ounces) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (26 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups diced, cooked chicken brest meat
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • Â½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 (9 ounce) packages frozen egg nonodels

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Combine chicken broth, both condensed soups, and diced chicken in a large pot. Season with onion powder, seasoning salt, and garlic powder.
  3. Bring a boil over high heat and stir in noodles.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer until desired consistency is reached, 20 to 30 minutes.
  5. Server hot and enjoy!
